*Obsolete Shinko Products

With the majority of industry moving across to the more sophisticated digital temperature controller Shinko decided it was time to stop the manufacture of many of their older products.

These include :

Analogue controllers
  • NCS-110, NCS-120 series
  • DIC-P, DIC-PO series
  • ACN-100, DCN-100 series
  • RC-400 series
  • AF-100 series
  • MC-100 series

Digital controllers
  • EC series
  • VC series
  • GC series
  • FIR-101, FIR-201 series
  • FCL-100 series

Testemp still offer a repair service for many of this products where components are not obsolete. Testemp are also willing to repair other manufacturers controllers, again where components are not obsolete.
