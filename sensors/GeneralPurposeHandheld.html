General Purpose Handheld Sensor

General Purpose Handheld Sensor Illustration

Technical Details

General Description

A hand held sensor designed generally to check the temperature of products

Sheath Material

  • 316 grade stainless steel (closed end)
  • flat or round tips available

Handle Material

  • Generally plastic but other materials e.g. nylon or stainless steel also available

Sensing Options

  • Thermocouple (normally K or T) insulated or grounded
  • Pt100 resistance
  • Thermistor

Cable

  • These style of sensors are normally supplied with straight or retractable curly cables
  • This is normally dependant upon the temperature measurement requirements
  • -10 to +105°C PVC
  • -50 to +250°C PFA / PTFE

Termination

  • Normally either stripped tails or a suitable connector to fit a mating hand held instrument.
