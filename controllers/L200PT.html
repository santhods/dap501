L200-PT Pt100 Resistance Thermometer Data Logger

L200 PT100 Data Logger

Specification

An 8-channel self calibrating data logger with up to 0.01C resolution. USB PC interface + software. CE Marked. ROHS compliant.

The Pt100 3 wire thermometer can be used in conjunction with a PC to provide accurate, versatile 8 channel measurement, scanning and logging of measured values. It can also be used as a "stand alone" indicator / logger and incorporates a digital display of measured temperature.

Self calibration of Pt100 ranges is simple and uses plug-in precision resistors.

The unit is designed to provide exceptional stability with high measurement resolution and represents an ideal crossover between plant practicality and laboratory performance at a very competitive price.

The PC software supplied with the instrument allows control, measure and calibration functions.
