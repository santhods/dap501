Shinko JCS Series

Shinko JCS-33A Series

Technical Details

Supply

  • Supply 85-264Vac 50/60Hz , 20-28Vac/dc 50/60Hz

18 Types of Input

  • thermocouple (10 types), RTD (2 types), DC current (2 types) and DC voltage (4 types)

3 Types of Output

  • Relay contact 3A 250Vac (resistive load), 1A 250Vac (inductive load)
  • Non-contact DC voltage : 12-14Vdc Max. 40mA (short circuit protected)
  • DC current : 4 to 20mA load resistance Max 550 ohms

Alarm Output

  • Relay contact 3A 250Vac (resistive load)

Options

  • Alarm 2 output : (not available on DCL)
  • Relay contact 3A 250Vac (resistive load)
  • Heater Break Alarm : (not available on JCL)
  • Current transformer detects burnout
  • Heater rated current 5A, 10A, 20A and 50A.
  • Setting accuracy---Within ±5% of heater rated current
  • Output : Relay contact 3A 250Vac (Resistive load)

Control output 2 to enable Heating/Cooling control (not available on JCL or DCL)

Serial Commns

  • Based on EIA, RS-485
  • Protocol : Based on Shinko standard protocol or Modbus (Selectable by key operation)
  • Number of connectable units : A maximum of 31 units per host computer

  • Transmitter Power Supply : (not available on JCL, JCS or DCL)
  • Output voltage: 24Vdc ±3V (When load current is 30mA)
