BTC Analogue Temperature Controllers

BTC Analogue Temperature Controllers, 2017

Technical Details

Models

  • BTC901 48mm wide x 48mm high x 86mm
  • BTC404 96mm wide x 96mm high x 53mm

Input Types

  • J thermocouple 0-200°C, 0-400°C, 0-600°C
  • K thermocouple 0-100°C, 0-200°C, 0-400°C, 0-600°C, 0-800°C
  • Pt100 0-100°C, 0-200°C, 0-300°C, 0-400°C

Supply

  • BTC901 100-130Vac 50/60Hz or 200-240Vac 50/60Hz
  • BTC404 90-240Vac 50/60Hz

Control

  • Generally on/off but proportional also available

Output Relay

  • contact 5A 250Vac (resistive load)

Alarm Output

  • Relay contact 2A 250Vac (resistive load) BTC404 0-100°C & 0-400°C only
